What is Detoura?

Detoura is Orlando Florida's premier melodic thrash metal band. The style of Detoura is one of seamless balance between the hooks of rock and the ferocity of metal. There is something in our music for everyone. Formed in 2002, Detoura has graced Orlando Florida with some one of the greatest metal experiences the city has to offer. The band has seen many iterations, but founder and front man Drew Trace has persisted through the highs and lows to help bring Detoura to where it is today. Bands like Detoura age like a fine wine, with each EP and album adding another dimension to an already solid sound. The musical sound of Detoura consists of melodic hooks, powerful choruses, intricate musicality, shredding guitars and aggressive screams. The music itself walks the fine line between metal and rock thus allowing Detoura’s music to reach a vast audience of musical tastes. Detoura’s line up consists of frontman Drew Trace, lead guitarist Zack Robin, bassist Roach Rose and drummer Shaun Kimmerling.
